Issues Resolved by the Service

Difficulty Scheduling Appointments

Potential clients may find it challenging to schedule appointments, especially if they need to register on an aggregator platform. Our product simplifies the process, allowing clients to choose the desired service and schedule an appointment in just a minute, without registering on the website.

Lack of Online Presence

Many independent professionals may lack the technical skills or resources to create and promote their own website. Our product provides them with a free and easy builder they can use to create their own website and promote their services online.

Difficulty Managing Clients

Independent professionals may face challenges managing clients, including contact information, payment details, and meeting schedules. Our product provides professionals with a personal dashboard to store and manage client data, simplifying tracking the needs and preferences of their clients.

Lack of Community and Learning Opportunities

Independent professionals may feel isolated and have limited opportunities for learning and career development. Our product offers a community of professionals with whom they can communicate and share knowledge and experience. It also provides learning centers to help professionals improve their skills and find new job opportunities.
